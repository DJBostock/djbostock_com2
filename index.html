About Me

Early in my working life I spent several years working on the production floor of a factory. One of the things that I learned there that is most applicable to my career in tech was a lot of practice with troubleshooting. Things would go wrong from time to time and often I would be the first person to try to figure out the what and why, and of course if I couldn't fix it then I would have to describe the issue to the maintanence person who would be next in line to diagnose the issue.

I have several years of experience working in tech support, and have earned a certificate in web development.
